HOW TO PLAY?

  1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND score ("CURRENT").
  3. IF the player rolls '1' he loses all of his ROUND points. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  4. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his MAIN score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  5. The first player to reach 100 points on MAIN score wins the game.

GOOD LUCK!
